Founding Members

Founding members of the Deutscher Werkbund were both artists and companies.

Artist

Peter Behrens , Rudolf Bosselt , Friedrich Deneken , Fritz Helmuth Ehmcke , Theodor Fischer , Josef Hoffmann , Wilhelm Kreis , Max Laeuger , Adelbert Niemeyer , Joseph Maria Olbrich , Karl Ernst Osthaus , Bruno Paul , Richard Riemerschmid , Jakob Julius Scharvogel , Paul Schultze-Naumburg , Fritz Schumacher , Wilhelm Thiele , Heinrich Vogeler .

Companies

Silverware factory Peter Bruckmann & Sons , German workshops for craftsmanship Dresden , Eugen Diederichs , Gebr. Klingspor , art printing artists' association Karlsruhe , Poeschel & Trepte , Saalecker workshops , United workshops for arts and crafts (Munich), workshops Bernard Stadler Paderborn , workshops for German household goods from Theophil Müller (Dresden), Wiener Werkstätte , Wilhelm & Co. (Munich), Gottlob Wunderlich (Waldkirchen-Zschopenthal).
